IEEE VR 2018: the 25th IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces

March 18-22, 2018, Reutlingen, Germany

http://ieeevr.org/2018/

IEEE VR 2018, the 25th IEEE Conference on Virtual Reality and 3D User Interfaces will be held from March 18th through March 22nd, 2018 at the Stadthalle Reutlingen in Reutlingen, Germany.

Tentative Registration fees for IEEE VR 2018 (last updated Nov. 28th)

Registration is now open! Click here to register!

Also, click here to book your room. Rooms are reserved until March 1st. If you wait, you may end up with a hotel far away from the venue.

You must register for the conference to receive a VISA letter. There is a check box in the registration process that will then ask for more information needed for the VISA letter. Please fill this out promptly and our registration administration will as quickly as possible send you a VISA letter.

Registration Types

Full Week:

USB Proceedings, Tutorials & Workshops (Sun-Mon), VR papers and panels sessions (Tues-Thurs), Videos, Demos VR Contest ( Mon-Thurs, Tournament: Wed Night), Two Keynote Talks (Tues & Thurs), Exhibitors Reception (Tues Lunch), Two Institute Receptions (Max Planck Tübingen AND Fraunhofer Stuttgart) (Mon and Tues Night), VR Banquet (Wed Night)

Main VR Only:

USB Proceedings, VR talk and panel sessions (Tues-Thurs), Two Keynote Talks (Tues & Thurs), Exhibitors Reception (Tues Lunch), One Institute Reception (Max Planck Tübingen OR Fraunhofer Stuttgart, choice will be given on a first-come first serve basis) (Tues night), Exhibitors Reception (Tues Lunch), VR Banquet (Wed night)

Tutorials, & Workshops Only:

USB Proceedings, All Tutorials & Workshop sessions (Sun-Mon), Videos, Demos and VR Contest (Mon, first day-only), One Institute Reception (Max Planck Tübingen OR Fraunhofer Stuttgart, choice will be given on a first-registration first serve basis) (Mon night)

One Day Conference Only:

USB Proceedings, Access to Technical Sessions, Exhibits, and Breaks for One Day Only. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately.

One Day Exhibits Only:

Access to the Exhibits Venue, Research Demos, and Breaks for One Day Only, Does not include assess to technical sessions. If applicable, a ticket to the day’s social event must be purchased separately. If multi-day exhibits access is desired, additional one day passes must be purchased onsite.
